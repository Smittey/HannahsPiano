Hi, I'm Hannah, pleased to meet you!


I am a friendly music graduate from Newcastle University currently working as an autistic teacher in Surrey. I also have a pet portrait business where I sell and draw my own portraits, and do like to draw the occasional Disney character! I have been teaching piano and music theory lessons for 2 years and teach all ages and abilities up to grade 5. I have experience preparing and entering students into ABRSM exams and can also offer extra A-level or GCSE Music tuition. I aim to be a friendly face to the lessons and hope to inspire a new skill and passion in the students I teach. I believe a calm learning environment is more beneficial to both teacher and pupil and I have an enthusiastic approach to teaching to help encourage students to learn and practice. For the younger learners I use more engaging techniques such as a mini manuscript white board, iPad apps and music games and reward stickers to keep learning fun and alive. Even with the youngest beginners during piano lessons I will incorporate some aspects of music theory and take the lesson further than what the books have to offer.
